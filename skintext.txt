<h1>Essential Oils for Skin Problems</h1>
<p>A lesser known fact about essential oils is that they can be a natural method for dealing with typical skin problems, <br>ranging from blemishes, redness, and dark circles. They should never be used as a substitute for prescription skincare products and always applied with a carrier oil.<br> Carrier oils are used to dilute the more potent essential oils and "carry" them to your skin.<br> Some popular examples are grapeseed oil and coconut oil.<br>Learn more about essential oils for skin care <a href="https://www.healthline.com/health/essential-oils-for-skin#dry-skin">here</a>.
